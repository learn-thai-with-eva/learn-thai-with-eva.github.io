I am Teacher Eva

Sawadee ka. My name is Eva, I’m from Roi-et. I’m currently studying English for Business Communication Bachelor Degree at Sripatum University in Bangkok.
I am native Thai and fluent in the English language. I am passionate about learning languages and would love to help you to learn Thai.

About My Lessons

  • Adapt to your Goals

    I create lessons that adapt to your goals. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced student, I can help you improve your speaking, listening, reading and writing skills.

  • Step by Step

    This course will give you a solid foundation so that you can use your Thai with confidence and enjoy the process even more.

  • Speak like Native

    My lessons will teach you how native actually speak, what they sound like, how to do it right and correct yourself when you make mistakes.
